About Me

Well what can I say...

I have been involved in website design and development since the late 90s. I have battled with Netscape Communicator, table layouts and shims and thrive off the developments and progression the industry has made over the years.

I currently wear three hats in my work; Digital Director of a small agency, a Front End specialist and friendly office janitor! It is safe to say I have had to become quite the generalist to cover these roles. If you are interested you can read more about my specific skills and experience here.

My original passion is illustration, mainly pen and ink. I am also a member of the Birmingham Printmakers and try to experiment with screen printing when I can.

I enjoy writing, although my quality is probably debateable! Nevertheless I have written some articles for Creativebloq and .Net magazine amongst others.
